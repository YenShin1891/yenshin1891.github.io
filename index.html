Yen Shin (신예은)

I am an undergraduate student at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), majoring in Computer Science and Science and Technology Policy.
From spring 2026, I will start my M.S. in Computer Science at KAIST, advised by Prof. Alice Oh.

Broadly, I am interested in climate action with machine learning and the social impacts of AI. I like questions such as: How can ML help us evaluate environmental and climate policies? Who is left out or harmed when LLMs are trained and deployed the way they are today?

  • Using ML models to emulate and extend integrated assessment models (IAMs) for climate policy.
  • Algorithmic fairness of LLMs, especially for marginalized groups.
  • Linking technical ML work with policy, governance, and human rights perspectives.
